TOWER PROGRESS

01 — Concept

It's All About Circuit Logic

So here's the deal with Circuit Tower — it's basically a puzzle game where you work your way up.
Every level is a self-contained electrical system. You must route power, balance signals, and activate components in the correct order.

  • There are no random solutions.
  • Every board has a logical path.
  • Solve it — and the tower grows.

02 — Gameplay

How It Works

  • 01

    Analyze the Board

    Identify inputs, outputs, and connection points. Observe how components interact.

  • 02

    Route the Current

    Rotate, align, and connect pathways to complete the circuit.

  • 03

    Activate the Platform

    Once the system is logically complete, power flows. The next platform unlocks.

03 — Progression

Structured Vertical Progression

The tower represents your progress. Each solved board becomes the foundation for the next.

Early platforms introduce simple routing. Higher levels combine signal timing, split currents, and conditional logic.

Yeah, it gets harder, but in a good way — not the kind that makes you want to throw your phone.
