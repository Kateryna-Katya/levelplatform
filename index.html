Solve the logic. Unlock the next board. Climb higher.

TOWER PROGRESS

01 — Concept

It's All About Circuit Logic

So here's the deal with Circuit Tower — it's basically a puzzle game where you work your way up.
Every level is a self-contained electrical system. You must route power, balance signals, and activate components in the correct order.

  • There are no random solutions.
  • Every board has a logical path.
  • Solve it — and the tower grows.

02 — Gameplay

How It Works

  • 01

    Analyze the Board

    Identify inputs, outputs, and connection points. Observe how components interact.

  • 02

    Route the Current

    Rotate, align, and connect pathways to complete the circuit.

  • 03

    Activate the Platform

    Once the system is logically complete, power flows. The next platform unlocks.

03 — Progression

Structured Vertical Progression

The tower represents your progress. Each solved board becomes the foundation for the next.

Early platforms introduce simple routing. Higher levels combine signal timing, split currents, and conditional logic.

Yeah, it gets harder, but in a good way — not the kind that makes you want to throw your phone.

07 — FAQ

Frequently Asked

  • It scales with you. Early platforms teach you the fundamentals — basic routing, simple connections. As you climb, complexity increases gradually. The difficulty is intentional and designed to be satisfying, not frustrating.

  • No prior engineering knowledge is required. The game teaches its logic visually and interactively. Once you start messing around with it, things start to click pretty naturally. Over time, you naturally understand more complex systems.

  • Standard mode does not force time pressure. You can think through each board carefully. Some optional challenge modes may include efficiency scoring or timed puzzles. The main game stays chill and lets you think things through.

  • Yes, progression is vertical and structured. Each platform unlocks only after the previous one is solved. This creates a clear sense of advancement. Complexity builds consistently across the tower.

  • The main experience focuses on personal mastery. However, efficiency ratings and optional leaderboards allow comparison. Players who enjoy optimization can aim for cleaner solutions. You can compete if you want, but you don't have to.
