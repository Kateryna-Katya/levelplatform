TOWER PROGRESS

01 — Concept

It's All About Circuit Logic

So here's the deal with Circuit Tower — it's basically a puzzle game where you work your way up.
Every level is a self-contained electrical system. You must route power, balance signals, and activate components in the correct order.

  • There are no random solutions.
  • Every board has a logical path.
  • Solve it — and the tower grows.
